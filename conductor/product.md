# Initial Concept
A portable Python time tracking application for managing work hours, overtime, vacation days, sick leaves, and work activities.

# Product Guide

## Vision Statement
Waqt is designed to be the ultimate personal time management companion for remote workers and employees. It provides a seamless, private, and highly efficient way to track work-life balance without the need for complex cloud services.

## Target Audience
- **Remote Workers and Employees:** Individuals who prioritize privacy and efficiency in managing their own work-life balance, tracking hours, and monitoring overtime for personal or professional reporting.

## Core Goals
- **Accurate Time Tracking:** Ensure precise logging of work hours and breaks to help users strictly adhere to their target workday (e.g., 8 hours).
- **Overtime Management:** Automatically calculate and visualize overtime hours to provide clear, actionable insights into extra work performed.
- **Integrated Leave Management:** Provide a unified view for tracking vacation days and sick leave alongside regular work hours to ensure comprehensive time awareness.

## Key Features
- **Interactive Timer Dashboard:** A robust real-time timer with Start, Stop, Pause, and Resume controls for effortless daily tracking.
- **Comprehensive Reporting:** Detailed weekly and monthly summaries that aggregate worked hours, overtime, and leave status.
- **Privacy & Portability:** A self-contained application using SQLite for local storage, ensuring user data remains private and the app remains portable across different environments.
- **Glassmorphism UI:** A modern, professional, and visually stunning interface that emphasizes clarity through transparency and depth.

## User Experience Focus
- **High Efficiency:** Prioritizing quick actions and low friction for common tasks like starting/stopping timers and logging activities.
- **Maximum Reliability:** Focused on data integrity and precise calculations to ensure users can trust their tracking history and overtime reports.
- **Modern Aesthetic:** Utilizing a glassmorphism design to create a professional and delightful user experience.
